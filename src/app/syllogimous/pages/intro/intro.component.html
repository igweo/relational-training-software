Welcome to RFT Trainer!

RFT Trainer takes classic logic challenges and turns them into a brain workout. This cognitive training platform introduces features designed to enhance your reasoning and problem-solving abilities.

RFT Trainer is based on Relational Frame Theory (RFT), a psychological theory that suggests that the fundamental process of human thought involves forming relationships between concepts. Studies indicate that training in relational skills can significantly enhance cognitive functions.

Therefore, by using RFT Trainer, you're boosting your problem-solving skills in a structured, evidence-based way.
